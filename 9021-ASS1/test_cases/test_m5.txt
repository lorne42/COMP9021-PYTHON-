Sir John said: "Sir Coral or Sir Sandy is a Knave!"
Sir Johnny said: "Sir Johnson, I, Sir August or Sir Jackson is a Knight."
"I am a Knight," whispered Sir Sandy.
Sir Sandy claims: "at most one of Sir John and I is a Knave."
Sir Johnson said: "exactly one of Sir Siren, Sir August and Sir Coral is a Knight."
"At least one of Sir Jackson and I is a Knave," whispered Sir August.
Sir Coral said: "Sir Johnny or Sir Siren is a Knight!"
Sir Jackson shouts: "exactly one of us is a Knight."
Sir Siren said: "Sir Johnson and Sir Johnny are Knights."
